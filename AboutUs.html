Mission :
To learn, discover, create, excel and make the parents, teachers and school proud.


Vision : 
Empowering students to fulfil their academic potential in a place that is diverse, welcoming and inclusive for all students and parents 

To in calculate higher order thinking skills among students and equip them with necessary thinking tools to enable them not only to perform well in all school and public exams but also guiding them to find solutions to all questions that confronts them.

To create leadership skills and generate knowledge through creative and innovative endeavours which provide a foundation for dealing with the needs of society.

Our Guiding Values :

Aim high

Be inclusive, treat each other with dignity and respect and promote patriotism 

Be accountable for our actions and strive to excel and produce the best results possible 

Value excellence, quality, service
