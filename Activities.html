Why Be Smarts ?

Today, we live in a multi-racial/ multi-national society. With this in mind, Be Smarts has always emphasised the significance of unity amongst people from different class and culture. Where better to start than in a
'children's garden' ?
Inculcating the necessity to live in peace and harmony should begin early - the formative years of a child.
